Bonsai
Project to automate the design of bonsai trees
Outline
1. Produce 3D point cloud of tree
2. Separate or remove the leaves from the wood
3. Model the trunk and branch structure
4. Use AI / ML to suggest initial tree shaping branch cuts based on:
	rules of traditional artistic principles of bonsai
	training on photographs of existing bonsai
	note: can only remove branches not add them
5. Produce image of resulting bonsai and digitally age it 5 years
